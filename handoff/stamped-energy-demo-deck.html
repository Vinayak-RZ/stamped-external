What Stamped actually does

From a plant signal to an assigned job.

Stamped does not replace EMS or EnMS. It runs the Connect → Observe → Decide → Execute → Verify loop: read existing data, issue a ranked ₹ action, hand it to the floor, and close it on the next bill.
