Technology

The stack behind each prescription

01 · Physics

Versioned industrial physics & rulepacks

Semver rule packs that encode affinity laws, holding floors, and tariff arithmetic — every finding cites a pack a plant EE can open.

  • MD coincidence, furnace holding, mill idle, compressed air, tariff dispatch
  • PF and tariff arithmetic tied to utility line items
  • Physics-informed hybrids; PINN research lineage, not black-box PDEs
Open deep dive →
02 · Models

Plant-calibrated industrial ML

Baselines and ranking models fitted on your plant’s telemetry — production-normalised residuals, not a chatbot on meter dumps.

  • TOW-P baselines · EWMA / CUSUM residual SPC
  • LightGBM quantile MD exceedance ranking
  • TimesFM-class foundation models in shadow only
Open deep dive →
03 · Agents

Bounded prescription agents

Agents draft floor actions with tools and vetoes — what, why, who, effort, ₹, due — while the calculation engine owns the money path.

  • Ranks by ₹ × confidence / effort
  • Physics and tariff vetoes are non-overridable
  • LLM for prose only — never invents ₹
Open deep dive →
04 · Evidence

Verified with evidence

In-house calculation engine recomputes ₹ from telemetry deltas and tariff lines, then checks whether the action actually moved the needle.

  • Potential vs realised vs remaining opportunity
  • M&V plan and baseline locked at issue time
  • Evidence trail for plant, finance, and assurance
